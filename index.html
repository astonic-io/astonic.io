The Mission

Astonic is built to facilitate the digital financial landscape by making decentralized stable currencies usable for everyone. Because of its decentralisation and easy-to-use protocol we empower communities everywhere to adopt our $aUSD stablecoin. With most payments and adoption moving to phones, Planq Network facilitates a mobile-first blockchain to create these new opportunities in the digital economy.

Core Building Blocks

Decentralization

The Astonic Platform and its tokens are governed by smart contracts. Ownership of the platform is in the hands of the community.

Stability Through Diversity

Expanding the range of assets in the Astonic Reserve collateral minimizes risk exposure and enhances the platform's overall resilience and reliability.

Building Trust Through Openness

Transparency is the foundation of trust, and Astonic embodies this principle at its core. With open-source code and publicly accessible reserve holdings, Astonic ensures complete accountability.

Protection

The Astonic Protocol incorporates advanced safeguards such as circuit breakers and trading limits, providing transparent, adaptive, and user-focused security measures.

Platform Features

Stable Asset Reserve

Astonic $aUSD is backed by over-collateralized, high-quality reserves. This offers you stability and trust, while not compromising on centralisation.
